Charlie Anthony Mejia

Lighting Designer

Charlie Anthony Mejia is a Bay Area–based Lighting Designer and Assistant Lighting Designer whose work blends theatrical storytelling with atmosphere, texture, and movement. His design style emphasizes emotionally driven environments and the relationship between light, music, and space, with a focus on creating worlds that feel immersive and alive.

Charlie’s work spans regional theatre, opera, and educational productions. Credits include collaborations with the San Francisco Opera and Berkeley Repertory Theatre, reflecting a practice grounded in collaboration and strong visual storytelling.

Recent projects include Pictures From Home at Marin Theatre and Annie at Berkeley Playhouse. He holds a BA in Drama from San Francisco State University.
